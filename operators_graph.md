## Operator calculus
-  Weighted difference (or weighted graph derivative) operator:

\begin{equation}
\partial_{j}\mathbf{x}_{i} = w_{i,j}^{\frac{1}{2}}(\mathbf{x}_{j}-\mathbf{x}_{i})
\end{equatio}
